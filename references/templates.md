# Self-Introduction Templates by Role Type

All templates follow the three-section structure: **Background → Capabilities → Motivation**.

---

## Engineering / Technical Roles

### 1 minute (ZH)

```
[背景]
面试官好，我叫[Name]，[N]年[领域]开发经验，目前在[Company]担任[Role]，主要负责[核心职责]。

[能力]
在专业上，我擅长[核心技能1]和[核心技能2]。我曾主导过[具体项目]，将[指标]从[X]提升到[Y]。我也具备[辅助技能]能力，曾通过[具体方法/行动]，[达成量化成果]。

[动机]
我一直关注贵公司在[具体产品/技术方向]上的投入。了解到团队正在[JD提到的方向]，这也是我下一阶段的核心目标，非常期待能加入您的团队。
```

### 1 minute (EN)

```
[Background]
Hi, I'm [Name], a [backend/frontend/etc.] engineer with [N] years of experience, currently at [Company] where I [current role and scope].

[Capabilities]
I specialize in [core skill 1] and [core skill 2]. I've led [specific project] that [quantified result — e.g. cut latency from X to Y]. I also bring [secondary strength] — for example, I [specific action] that [quantified outcome].

[Motivation]
I've been following [Company]'s work on [specific product/tech], especially [specific detail]. When I saw this role focuses on [JD keyword], I knew it aligned with where I want to take my career. I'd love to contribute.
```

## Quick Checklist

Before using any template, verify:
1. **Every bracket replaced** with a real, specific detail (not a vague category)
2. **At least one number** in the Capabilities section
3. **Motivation references something real** about the company — a product, a tech choice, a team initiative
4. **Read it aloud** — if it sounds like you're reading a script, rewrite until it doesn't
